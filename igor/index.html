Example Club is a small, community-driven chess club located in the heart of the city. Our mission is to promote the game of chess and bring people together to share their passion for the timeless game.

Our weekly meetings are an opportunity for members to play casual games, learn from each other, and participate in friendly competitions. We also offer lessons and workshops for those who want to improve their game, as well as organized tournament events for those who enjoy a more competitive environment.

In addition to our weekly meetings, Example Club is also active in the larger chess community. We participate in local tournaments, support chess programs in local schools, and work with other clubs to promote the game of chess in our community.
